BRAND IDENTITY
- Name: Washly
- Tagline: "Fresh clothes, zero effort."
- Tone: Friendly, energetic, trustworthy, modern
- Colors: Use a colorful, vibrant palette — primary coral/orange (#FF6B4A), secondary sky blue (#4AB8FF), accent mint green (#3DDBB5), neutral white/light gray backgrounds. Use gradients and bold color blocks liberally.
- Typography: Rounded, modern sans-serif (e.g. Inter, Nunito, or Poppins). Large headlines, friendly body text.
- Aesthetic: Clean and airy with pops of color. Think consumer app meets lifestyle brand. Inspired by Washmen, Laundryheap, and Rinse.

PAGE STRUCTURE & SECTIONS

1. NAVIGATION BAR
- Logo ("Washly" in bold rounded font with a small bubble/sparkle icon)
- Nav links: How It Works, Services, Pricing, About
- CTA button: "Book a Pickup" (coral/orange, rounded pill shape)
- Sticky on scroll

2. HERO SECTION
- Bold headline: "Laundry Day is Cancelled." or "Your Cleanest Clothes Yet."
- Subheadline: "We pick up, clean, and deliver your laundry — next day."
- Two CTA buttons: "Book a Pickup" (primary) + "See How It Works" (ghost/outline)
- Trust badges row below CTAs: ⭐ 4.9 Rating | 🚗 Free Pickup | ⏱ 24hr Turnaround | 🌿 Eco-Friendly

- Colorful abstract blob shapes or wavy background accents

3. HOW IT WORKS (3 steps)
- Section title: "As easy as 1, 2, 3"
- Step 1: "Schedule a Pickup" — choose your time in the app or website
- Step 2: "We Clean Everything" — professional cleaning at our facility
- Step 3: "Delivered to Your Door" — fresh, folded, and on time
- Each step has a large colorful icon, step number, and short description
- Use a horizontal card layout on desktop, vertical stack on mobile

4. SERVICES SECTION
- Section title: "Everything You Need, Cleaned"

  • Bedding & Linens — sheets, duvets, towels
- Each card uses a different accent color from the palette
- "View Full Pricing" link below

5. PRICING SECTION
- Section title: "Simple, Transparent Pricing"

6. TESTIMONIALS

7. APP DOWNLOAD CTA (if applicable)

8. FAQ SECTION

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll animations (fade-in on scroll for sections)
- Rounded corners everywhere (cards, buttons, images — border-radius: 16–24px)
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Use colorful SVG illustrations or emoji-style icons (not generic flat icons)
- Avoid stock-photo clichés — use modern, diverse lifestyle imagery descriptions
- Micro-interactions: button hover states, card hover lifts (box-shadow), smooth transitions
